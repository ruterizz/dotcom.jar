About Ruter1z
Indie J2ME / Java phone game developer.

Hey! I'm Ruter1z (Rainers), a 15-year-old indie developer focused on creating games for old-school J2ME / Java phones.

I love retro mobile technology, classic button phones, and the unique limitations of early mobile gaming. Instead of making modern Android or PC games, I experiment with tiny resolutions, keypad controls, pixel art, and lightweight Java ME apps — the same style used on legendary phones from the 2000s.

Most of my projects are inspired by retro games, survival mechanics, arcade gameplay, and the feeling of old Nokia-era mobile gaming. I enjoy learning how classic mobile devices worked and pushing J2ME farther than most people expect today.

Besides game development, I also like:

  • Retro phones & mobile tech
  • Java ME / MIDP development
  • Pixel art
  • Modding and experimenting with old software
  • Preserving classic mobile gaming culture

My goal is to keep old mobile gaming alive and create new experiences for devices people thought were forgotten.

Thanks for checking out my projects :)
